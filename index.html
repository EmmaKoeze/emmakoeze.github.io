Bio

I’m a wind turbine parts trader based in Rotterdam, the Netherlands.

Growing up on a farm in Central Virginia it was easy to see the stars. Still, I only found out much later in life that Orian’s Belt isn’t an asterism in the Big Dipper and meteoroids become meteorites only if they don't burn up in the atmosphere.

Back in the day I always wanted to taste every fig that grew on the tree just like the next person. But I realized that sometimes you just have to pick and eat. So here are the fruits in my basket.
